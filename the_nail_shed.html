Hi, I'm Tati.

I've been doing nails for over a decade, and The Nail Shed is the private studio I built to do them my way: one client at a time, by appointment, with no overlapping sessions. Every set begins with a conversation about what you want, what'll wear with your life, and what shape suits your hands. Then a couple of unhurried hours.
